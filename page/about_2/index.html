Here I Am

Hello there. Welcome. I find it strange that you are here, reading this. What does it say about you that you are? What does it say about me that I even wrote this in the first place?

I am just a lowly data scientist who put this site together to provide materials to students who hope to learn something about data science from me. There is no other reason for you to be here, reading this.

But you are here. And that says something. You are special, in a good way. You have initiative. You’re an explorer. You seek wisdom, beauty, and ways to pass the time. I hope that I can provide at least two out of those three things for you.

About Me

I’m a father first. Husband second. Beyond that, I am me, scurrying around daily scratching a variety of mental, physical, and emotional itches. Consuming In-N-Out burgers, popular radio, streaming media, the golden age of television, and fossil fuels. Always looking for enjoyment in the present whilst fretting about the future. The past is distant. Nothing more than a faded old photograph, an unfocused 8mm film with poor sound…

Okay, enough pretentiousness. I am, for your purposes, a data scientist, and have been since 2011. I have a PhD in Statistics and have worked as a data scientist in the Bay Area at several companies. I am legit.
